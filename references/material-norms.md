# Material Norms for Apartment Renovation Estimates

Starting norms for draft material takeoff. Not a supplier catalog and not a price list.

General rules: add 3-10% waste, round purchasable units up, keep consumption text visible, do not include finish products selected by the client unless requested.

Examples: protective film 1.1 m2 per protected m2; construction bags 6-10 pcs per m3 debris; gas block 100x250x625 about 6.4 pcs per m2 plus 5%; adhesive for gas block 1.5-2.0 kg per m2; primer 0.1-0.2 kg per m2 per layer; tile adhesive 3-6 kg per m2; GKL and profile quantities are system-dependent and must use project/system details when available.

Electrical and plumbing cable/pipe lengths are route-based and must not be invented without routing assumptions.
