# Work Catalog for Apartment Renovation Estimates

Practical work sections and row patterns. Not a price list.

Standard sections: preparation and demolition, partitions, GKL and embedded structures, ceilings, floors, waterproofing, wall rough finish, wall finish, ceiling finish, flooring and skirting, doors/openings, plumbing, electrical, lighting, low-current, additional expenses.

Typical units: m2 for protection, demolition, partitions, ceilings, floors and finishes; m3 for debris and screed where needed; linear meters for profiles/skirting/routes; pieces for doors, points, fixtures and sanitary equipment; комплект for panels or package scopes.

Every row must be traceable to a PDF source, extracted quantity or explicit assumption.
