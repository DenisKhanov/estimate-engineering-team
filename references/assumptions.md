# Assumptions Policy for Apartment Estimates

Do not invent silently. Missing ceiling height, construction type, GKL layers, waterproofing extent, plaster thickness, cable routing, plumbing routing and exact fixture type must become explicit assumptions or questions.

Confidence levels: high for explicit schedules/notes, medium for reliable calculations, low for partial inference. Low confidence requires manual check.

Always flag visualization-only quantities, area mismatch above 2%, missing heights, unknown material systems, contractor-supplied packages and unverified exact ГЭСН/ФЕР codes.
