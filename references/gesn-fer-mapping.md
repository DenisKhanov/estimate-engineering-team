# GESN/FER Mapping Notes for Apartment Renovation

Official entry point: https://www.minstroyrf.gov.ru/trades/tsenoobrazovanie/federalnyy-reestr-smetnykh-normativov/

Use ГЭСН/ФЕР logic to justify units, included work scope and missing auxiliary operations. Do not fill prices from ФЕР unless the user explicitly asks. Do not invent exact code numbers.

Practical groups: demolition, masonry partitions, GKL partitions, GKL ceilings, screeds, waterproofing, plaster/putty, painting, tile works, doors, electrical points, plumbing points.

Red flags: mismatched units, wall finish area derived only from floor area, GKL ceilings without profiles, electrical points without routing assumptions, wet rooms without waterproofing, doors without openings/lintels where partitions are new.
